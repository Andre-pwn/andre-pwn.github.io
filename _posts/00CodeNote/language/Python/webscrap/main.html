Neighborhood

\n

Talison Row at Daniel Island has a beautiful location near the Cooper River with access to Interstate 526 and Downtown Charleston. Our community is close to Riverfront Park and Joe Riley Waterfront Park, which has beautiful fountains, views of dolphins at the harbor, and swings on the docks leading to the marsh. Foodies will marvel at the endless list of dining options, from Fleet Landing Restaurant and Bar to Circa 1886. Catch a concert at Volvo Car Stadium, grab drinks at Bin 526, take in a Charleston Battery soccer game at MUSC Health Stadium, visit Smythe Park or dine at Sermet\xe2\x80\x99s Courtyard. Theater lovers will have a ball at Footlight Players Theater and art enthusiasts can visit a variety of art museums including the Gibbes Museum of Art. Highly-rated Howe Hall Aims Elementary School, Marrington Elementary School, Daniel Island School, and Academic Magnet High School making it an ideal location for education with a short school commute. When you\xe2\x80\x99re hungry, you can trek less than a mile to Blondies Bagels and Cafe, known for their cozy atmosphere, bagels with lox, and lattes. With all of these schools, parks, and eateries within reach, our Charleston, South Carolina neighborhood welcomes you! Fun is also just around the corner from your home! Schedule a tour and experience our smoke-free community for yourself!
